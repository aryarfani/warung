How are our coffee made ?


Harvested by our experienced farmer who has immersed in the world of coffee for many years to make sure that every cup has the real taste of how coffee is.

The fineness of the grind strongly affects brewing. Brewing methods that expose coffee grounds to heated water for longer require a coarser grind than faster brewing methods.

Sophisticated technology is used to enchacne the best taste of what coffee could possible be. And in the hand of our skilled bartenders, all of your expectation will come into reality
